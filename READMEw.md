## Interview Tips

1. **Use STAR Method** for scenario questions:
   - Situation: Set the context
   - Task: What needed to be done
   - Action: What you did
   - Result: Outcome and lessons learned

2. **Be specific**: Use real examples from your experience

3. **Show problem-solving**: Explain your thought process

4. **Demonstrate leadership**: For 8+ years role, show team leadership examples

5. **Azure focus**: Emphasize Azure-specific implementations

6. **Simple English**: Explain complex concepts clearly
